[My Starred Places - Taste Profile]

1. Devoción (Brooklyn, NY): 
- Vibe: Industrial jungle. Huge skylight with lots of natural light, exposed brick walls, and a massive vertical garden.
- Why I like it: It feels spacious yet cozy. The raw, industrial aesthetic matches my creative energy.

2. Blue Bottle Coffee (Beverly Blvd, LA):
- Vibe: Minimalist & Clean. Very bright, white-toned, and extremely simple interior design. 
- Why I like it: I value efficiency and a distraction-free environment. The "less is more" approach is my favorite.

3. Death & Co (East Village, NY):
- Vibe: Dark, Moody, and Sophisticated. A legendary speakeasy with dim lighting and leather seating.
- Why I like it: I love intimate spaces for deep conversations. The dark and mysterious atmosphere feels very premium.

4. The Broad (Los Angeles, CA):
- Vibe: Modern Architectural Masterpiece. Geometric patterns, white honeycomb structure, and very artistic.
- Why I like it: I appreciate unique building stories and clean lines. I prefer places that feel like a gallery.

[General Preference Summary]
- Keywords: Industrial, Minimalist, Moody, Architectural.
- Dislikes: Loud music, generic tourist chains (like Starbucks), and overcrowded bright cafeterias.
